Install Laptop Display Components

LCD and OLED

  • Most monitors are LCD displays, along with laptops.
  • However, those need back light to work and the graphics aren't as good as OLED displays.
  • Other mobile devices, on the other hand, such as tablets and phones, have OLED screens to increase quality. They are quite expensive, which explains why mobile phones are as expensive as some laptops.

Media Parts

  • Many parts need to be removed to get to the mic, but check if the connecting cable is damaged (you most probably would fix that instead)
  • Inverters: circuit boards behind the LCD screen.
  • They take DC power to and from the LCD screen.
  • Digitizers: a thin film on touchscreens; they detect pressure to activate certain buttons
  • To replace them: heat the glue, detach carefully, replace with new digitizer
