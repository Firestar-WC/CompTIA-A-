Types of Mobile Devices

Tablets, Smartphones, Wearables

  • Most tablets run Windows, iOS (Apple), or Android
  • Tablets have a screen size of 8-13", can run with or without keyboards, and can use wireless or cellular.
  • Smartphones have a screen size of 4-6", can run iOS or Andriod, can play media, surf the Web, run apps, track activities, and generally be a phone.

Smart Watches and Fitness Monitors

  • Fitness monitors show and rack steps; they are generally built in on a smart watch.
  • Smart watches have improved in quality nowadays, and can support new additional apps, text, or even call.

VR/AR Headsets, E-Readers, and GPS

  • VR headsets (virtual reality) are used for gaming; AR headsets, on the other hand, are for training
  • E-readers are devices used to read e-books, however, with the development of e-book apps on mobile devices, they have been rendered useless (ex. Kindle, Nook)
  • Global Positioning System tells yuor current position and can give directions to other places when given that address.
  • For GPS to work, location services must be on.
